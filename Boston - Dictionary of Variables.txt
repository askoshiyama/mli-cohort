There are 14 attributes in each case of the dataset. They are:
V1 - CRIM - per capita crime rate by town
V2 - ZN - proportion of residential land zoned for lots over 25,000 sq.ft.
V3 - INDUS - proportion of non-retail business acres per town.
V4 - CHAS - Charles River dummy variable (1 if tract bounds river; 0 otherwise)
V5 - NOX - nitric oxides concentration (parts per 10 million)
V6 - RM - average number of rooms per dwelling
V7 - AGE - proportion of owner-occupied units built prior to 1940
V8 - DIS - weighted distances to five Boston employment centres
V9 - RAD - index of accessibility to radial highways
V10 - TAX - full-value property-tax rate per $10,000
V11 - PTRATIO - pupil-teacher ratio by town
V12 - B - 1000(Bk - 0.63)^2 where Bk is the proportion of blacks by town
V13 - LSTAT - % lower status of the population
T1 - MEDV - Median value of owner-occupied homes in $1000's
